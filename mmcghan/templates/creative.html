Creative Writing

A love of reading stories quickly grew into a desire to write them when I was still a young child. I received encouragement in high school when I placed high two years in a row in the National Scholastic Writing Awards—second prize in 10th grade and honorable mention in eleventh, out of roughly 20,000 entries from students across the country.

While a student UNLV's Creative Writing MFA Program, I studied fiction with Douglas Unger and poetry with Aliki Barnstone and Mark Irwin. I had poetry published in the literary magazines Many Mountains Moving and Red Rock Review. I also won first place in both the fiction and poetry categories of the Las Vegas CityLife poetry and fiction contest. Click the link below to read my winning entries.
