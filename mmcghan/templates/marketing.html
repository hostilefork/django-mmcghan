Arts Editor Position

The growing population of locals in the suburban communities of Henderson and Summerlin spurred the publisher of the tourist-oriented entertainment guide, What's On The Las Vegas Guide, to create two sister magazines to the original. While on staff at Las Vegas CityLife as calendar editor/staff writer, I was offered an editorial position on the two local magazines, which both came out on a monthly basis. I left CityLife to become the Arts Editor for What's On.

This was an in-house position in which I wrote copy, hired freelancers and assigned pieces to them, and copyedited other writers' work. I collected and updated listings for a comprehensive calendar of activities and attractions. I helped plan content for future issues, and picked up a bit of graphic design knowledge along the way. The staff was small, and as Arts Editor I reported directly to the Managing Editor. Our entire department consisted of five writer/editors and we employed about half a dozen local freelance writers.
