Technical Writing

Most of my technical writing has been for the construction and manufacturing industries, with forays here and there into other subjects. For me, domain matters less than access to knowledgeable experts and research materials, as well as awareness of who the audience is and the final form and use of the deliverables.

A selection of pages from this manual for carpenters is representative of textbooks I wrote for union apprentices.

While working for the Progressive Leadership Alliance of Nevada (PLAN), I wrote a document that described the experiences in restoring voting rights to ex-felons who had been honorably discharged. It outlines the program's successes and challenges, and makes recommendations for how the state could improve the process in the future: Voting Rights Restoration in Nevada 2008 (PDF)
